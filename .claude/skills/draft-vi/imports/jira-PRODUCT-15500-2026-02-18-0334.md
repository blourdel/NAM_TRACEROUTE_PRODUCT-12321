# PRODUCT-15500 - [Phase 3] Upgrade readiness flow

## Summary

Introduce an upgrade workflow for Phase 1 and Phase 2 customers, enabling them to transition their tenants to a fully 3rd Gen Dynatrace experience with simplified administration, enhanced scalability, and a modernized user interface.

## Problem Statement

Customers using hybrid tenants are unable to fully benefit from the scalability, simplified administration, and modernized user interface offered by 3rd Gen Dynatrace. Without a structured upgrade workflow, the transition process is unclear, leading to potential delays, misconfigurations, or incomplete migrations. Additionally, there is no mechanism to ensure readiness or provide support for the upgrade process, leaving customers uncertain about the steps required.

## Proposed Solution

### What It Is

A structured upgrade workflow that guides customers through the transition of their tenants to the latest 3rd Gen Dynatrace platform (Phase 3). This workflow includes clear steps, automated checks, and support resources to ensure a smooth migration process. The upgrade will enable customers to benefit from improved scalability, simplified administration, and a modernized user interface.

### Concept

The upgrade workflow is designed to be executed over weeks or months, with the following key components:

1. **Upgrade Notification**: Admins are notified about the option to upgrade to the 3rd Gen experience.
2. **Upgrade Guides and Dashboards**: Ready-made dashboards and detailed guides are provided to assist with the migration process.
3. **Customer and Dynatrace Actions**: Clearly define what actions are automated by Dynatrace and what steps are required from the customer.
4. **Readiness Checks**: Provide tools and metrics to help customers verify their readiness for the upgrade.
5. **Upgrade Execution**: Admins can "flip the switch" to upgrade their tenant, transitioning it to Phase 3.
6. **Reversion Option**: Admins can revert the upgrade temporarily, ensuring flexibility during the transition.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Upgrade Notification**: Admins in Phase 1 and Phase 2 tenants receive notifications about the upgrade option.
2. **Upgrade Guides**: Detailed documentation and ready-made dashboards are available to assist with the migration.
3. **Readiness Checks**: Tools and metrics are provided to verify tenant readiness for the upgrade.
4. **Upgrade Execution**: Admins can initiate the upgrade via the platform, transitioning their tenant to Phase 3.
5. **Reversion Capability**: Admins can revert the upgrade within a specified timeframe.
6. **Data Transition**: Classic data access and writes are phased out according to the defined workflow.
7. **Scalability Management**: Only eligible customers are notified and supported during the upgrade process.

## Additional Notes

* Ensure backend requirements for double writing of data are verified, and identify which data does not require double writing post-upgrade.
* Clearly communicate that Classic apps and functionality will not have additional removal steps beyond this workflow.
* Include links to step-by-step guides and best practices within the provided dashboards.
* Consider scalability limitations when determining which customers to notify and support during the upgrade.

## Tracking Requirements

* **Upgrade Adoption Rate**: Measure the percentage of tenants that successfully transition to Phase 3.
* **Readiness Check Completion**: Track how many customers complete readiness checks before upgrading.
* **Reversion Rate**: Track how often tenants revert the upgrade and the reasons for doing so.
* **Data Transition Metrics**: Measure the progress of data migration from Classic to 3rd Gen systems.

Note: Collect all these metrics on a self-monitoring dashboard.

This structured upgrade workflow ensures a seamless transition for customers, enabling them to fully leverage the benefits of the 3rd Gen Dynatrace platform.
