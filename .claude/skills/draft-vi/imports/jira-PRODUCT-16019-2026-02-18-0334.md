# PRODUCT-16019 - Generalize Platform Search service to AppShell service

**Status:** Usecases defined
**Type:** Technical enabler (infrastructure)
**Assignee:** Simon Ludwig
**Execution Assignee:** Damian Skrodzki
**Owning Program:** Platform Apps
**Fix Version:** Apr 2026
**Target End:** 2026-04-30
**Sprint:** Planned for 2026 CQ2
**Created:** 2026-01-12
**Updated:** 2026-02-11

## Summary

The Platform Search service responsibility could be extended to cover some backend for frontend use cases, including serving the AppShell app. We've collected 8 use cases where having such a service would make things easier, but it's always too big a step as a part of a feature-VI. We need this prerequisite to enable such an option for future development.

## Problem Statement

The current division and purpose of the AppShell and Platform Search service components don't align with our goals. We're organizationally limited in our ability to optimize performance and easily access internal services. It's technically possible, but we're only bound by the Platform Search service's responsibilities.

## Proposed Solution

Re-purpose (and rename) Platform Search service onto AppShell Service. The new AppShell service becomes a generic-purpose UI service used by AppShell and other PAPA use cases (when appropriate).

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Platform Search service becomes AppShell Service
2. (optional) AppShell Service versioning includes the Sprint version in the version number
